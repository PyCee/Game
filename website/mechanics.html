Mechanics

Knife

The Protagonist wields a knife which, when swung, lights up a small area.
If swung at special walls the knife will leave a glowing mark.

Close Eyes

 The Protagonist can close his eyes, allowing (P2) to control the body.

Half Blindness

 Part of side of screen is blacked out. Caused by one eye being stabbed by The Prince if you keep looking at him.

Light Adjustment

 If average light in sight is out of set range of brightness, ambiant light is adjusted
to bring the average brightness up/down into range.
